Gabby Laurens



About

Gabby Laurens is a florist in down town Houston. Her job is to take care of her uncle's flower shop while he is away on an emergency vacation. She resides in the apartment above the shop with her cat, Luna.

Age : 21

Hometown : Chicago

Favorite Artists : Beyoncè, Florence + the Machine, Adele, Shawn Mendes, Ed Sheeran

Favorite Color : Teal

Favorite Food : Pasta
