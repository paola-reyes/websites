Reading is my favorite hobby because I can lose myself in the world of books. My favorite books are all fiction - I get distracted easily with nonfiction. I have a preference for book series because it is easier to simply read the next book rather than having to find another book to read. Although sometimes I will settle for individual books, as they are actually that interesting and unfortunatley do not have a sequel or prequel. I am a strong supporter of reading the book before the movie, so I can be found with a book froma popular or recent movie. Take a look at some of my favorite book series and books I want to or plan to read.
